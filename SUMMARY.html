System Architecture Summary

Our solution implements a high-performance UDP transport protocol optimized for low latency and high reliability under lossy network conditions.

  • Jitter Buffer: We utilized a circular ring buffer with O(1) complexity to reorder out-of-order packets efficiently without the overhead of dynamic memory allocation.
  • Time-Aware Playout: By calculating absolute delivery deadlines using T0 and DELAY_MS, the receiver identifies and drops fundamentally lost packets early, successfully preventing "Head-of-Line" blocking.
  • Bandwidth Efficiency: We implemented NACK Batching, where multiple missing sequence numbers are packed into a single UDP feedback packet, ensuring our bandwidth overhead remains well below the 2.00x threshold even during significant packet loss.
  • Retransmission Logic: The sender maintains a history buffer that allows for instantaneous retransmission of missing frames, throttled by a cooldown timer to prevent network congestion.
